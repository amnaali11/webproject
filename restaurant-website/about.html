About Us

In the 1950s, all that stood in this iconic location was a car park with a small kiosk. Even then it was the best spot for uninterrupted panoramic views of Adelaide, with the address a tourist destination in itself. In October 1985, RavoRes as we know it today opened. Every detail was custom designed to maximise the benefits of this stunning location. Open all week from 10:30 AM until 12:00 PM. On Friday and Saturday until 1:00 AM. Kitchen open 11:30 AM until 10:30 PM. Fridays and Saturdays until 11:30 PM.
